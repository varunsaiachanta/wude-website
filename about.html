The Story Behind Wude

Founded in 2019, Wude was born from a simple yet powerful belief: that every space has the potential to inspire, transform, and enrich the lives of those who inhabit it. Our journey began when our founder, Sarah Mitchell, recognized that the world needed design solutions that were not only beautiful but also intelligent, sustainable, and truly personalized.

What started as a small design studio has grown into a comprehensive design firm that specializes in interior design, modular solutions, custom furniture, and landscape design. Our name "Wude" represents our philosophy of creating spaces that are both wise in their functionality and beautiful in their execution.

Today, we're proud to have transformed over 150 spaces across residential, hospitality, and commercial sectors, each project reflecting our commitment to excellence, innovation, and the unique vision of our clients.

Our Mission

To transform ordinary spaces into extraordinary experiences through innovative design, sustainable practices, and unwavering attention to detail. We believe that great design has the power to improve lives, foster connections, and create lasting memories.

Our Vision

To be the leading design studio that redefines how people think about and interact with their spaces. We envision a world where every environment is thoughtfully designed to enhance human experience, promote sustainability, and inspire creativity.
