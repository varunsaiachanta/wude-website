Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary depending on scope and complexity. A small residential project might take 4-8 weeks, while larger commercial projects can take 3-6 months or more. We'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

What is your design process?

Our process includes discovery & consultation, concept development, design & planning, implementation, and final touches. We work closely with clients throughout each phase to ensure their vision is realized.

Do you work with specific budgets?

Yes, we work with various budget ranges and can tailor our services accordingly. We're transparent about costs and will work with you to maximize value within your budget constraints.

Can you work with existing furniture?

Absolutely! We often incorporate existing pieces into new designs. We'll assess what works well and suggest what might need updating or replacing to achieve your desired aesthetic.

Do you provide 3D renderings?

Yes, we provide 3D renderings and visualizations to help you visualize the final result before implementation begins. This ensures we're aligned on the design direction.

What areas do you serve?

We primarily serve the San Francisco Bay Area, including San Francisco, Oakland, Berkeley, and surrounding cities. For larger projects, we may consider locations throughout Northern California.
